The Legacy of God of War 4

God of War 4 marked a bold new chapter in the iconic PlayStation series. Moving from Greek to Norse mythology, the game introduced a deeper and more emotional story for Kratos. With breathtaking visuals and seamless combat mechanics, it set new standards for action-adventure games. The bond between Kratos and his son Atreus added powerful storytelling depth rarely seen in the genre. Praised by fans and critics alike, God of War 4 stands as one of the greatest games of its generation.
